F.A.Q

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What are the course levels offered?

    We offer courses for all levels of English proficiency, from beginner to advanced. Our courses are designed to help students practice their language skills in real-life situations, and our teachers are trained to adapt the material to suit the needs of each level of student.

  • What is the class size?

    Our online classes typically have smaller class sizes, with a maximum of 10 students per class. We believe that this class size provides enough students to form pairs and groups for practice-based activities, while still allowing for personalized attention and instruction from the teacher.

  • How long is each class?

    Classes are typically 90 minutes long for adult students and 60 minutes long for younger learners.

  • What is the schedule of classes?

    Our courses have flexible schedules, with options for both weekday and weekend classes. Students can choose the schedule that best fits their needs.

  • Are there any prerequisites to enroll?

    There are no specific prerequisites to enroll, as we offer courses for all levels of English proficiency.

  • What equipment do I need to take this course?

    You need a laptop or a desktop computer to access our courses. Since we use online teaching technology in our lessons you may have problems with tablets and smartphones during the live lesson activities.

  • How do I access course materials?

    Course materials are provided through our online learning platform, accessible to students with an internet connection. Our materials can be accessed via Google Classroom, Ellii platform, and during the live lessons. We strive to provide a seamless and comprehensive learning experience for our students.

  • What is the duration of the courses?

    The duration of the courses will depend on the specific course and length of enrollment, with options ranging from a few weeks to several months. The duration of each class is defined before the registration.
